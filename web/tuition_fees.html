Tuition and Fees:

Starting 2016 – 2017 school year Roslyn Academic Programs calendar will be based on the quarter system. School year is divided into four (4) academic quarters of 8 weeks each (maybe more in some years). The last quarter of 2016-2017 school year is 9 weeks.

  1. Registration Fee: a non-refundable $50 fee per student (not per family). This is a one-time fee exclusively for NEW students. Application fee is waived for returning students. Fee should be paid in the time of the registration.
  2. Enrollment Fee:The fee is considered as a deposit towards thelast tuition payment (for last quarter) Enrollment fee is $85 from the family with one child, $170 – with 2 children, $235 – with 3 or more children. Returning students should pay enrollment fee at the time of re-registration (before September 10th 2015) and new students should pay it at the time of the 1st tuition payment (2nd week of the 1st quarter).
