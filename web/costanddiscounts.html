Tuition Cost and Discounts

Starting 2016 – 2017 school year the cost of tuition is:

  • One 60 minutes class: $35/class
  • Two 60 minutes classes: $30/class
  • Three and more 60 minutes classes $25/class

All further discounts will be decided on the individual basis and have to be approved by both Tatiana Portnaya and Marina Terentieva.
