Books and supplies

Starting 2016 – 2017 school year Roslyn Academic Programs calendar will be based on the quarter system. School year is divided into four (4) academic quarters of 8 weeks each (maybe more in some years). The last quarter of 2016-2017 school year is 9 weeks.

  1. Registration Fee: a non-refundable $50 fee per student (not per family). This is a one-time fee exclusively for NEW students. Application fee is waived for returning students. Fee should be paid in the time of the registration.
  2. Enrollment Fee:The fee is considered as a deposit towards thelast tuition payment (for last quarter) Enrollment fee is $85 from the family with one child, $170 – with 2 children, $235 – with 3 or more children. Returning students should pay enrollment fee at the time of re-registration (before September 10th 2015) and new students should pay it at the time of the 1st tuition payment (2nd week of the 1st quarter).
  3. Material Fee:Printed materials are provided by Roslyn Academic programs and bear no additional fee. Should any additional fee be required for some classes the corresponding teacher will discuss it directly with the parents.
  4. Administrative Fee:The fee of $30 from families with one child and $35 from families with two or more children covers different school activities, including foreign languages day, graduation and moving up ceremonies, cleaning after the events and a snack, served during each school day.
  5. Administrative Fee:The fee of $30 from families with one child and $35 from families with two or more children covers different school activities, including foreign languages day, graduation and moving up ceremonies, cleaning after the events and a snack, served during each school day.

Most of our classes provide printed materials and do not require a purchase of a textbook or a workbook. Should your child need a textbook or a workbook for a specific class, the teacher will discuss it with you individually. School provides books directly from distributors at a very good discounted price.
